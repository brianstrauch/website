Pizza 🍕

Day 1

2 3 4
water 💧 (90°F) 245g 368g 490g
sourdough starter 49g 74g 98g
flour 328g 491g 655g
whole wheat flour 23g 34g 45g
salt 🧂 7g 11g 14g

8:00 PM

  • Combine ingredients in bowl, mix dough for 5 minutes, cover with damp towel
  • Stretch and fold every 30 minutes for 1.5 hours
  • Cover and let sit for the remaining time

11:00 PM

  • Work into taught ball, place in oiled bowl
  • Refrigerate dough overnight

Day 2

4
tomatoes 🍅 28oz
olive oil 2 tbsp
salt 🧂 to taste

1:00 PM

  • Cut dough into 2 pieces
  • Shape dough into balls, place on baking sheet, and proof for 4 hours

4:00 PM

  • Preheat oven and pizza stone to 500
  • Drain tomatoes, add olive oil and salt, blend and cook sauce

5:00 PM

  • Shape dough, add ingredients
  • Bake for 5 minutes, broil for 3 minutes
